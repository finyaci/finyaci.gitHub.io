CET6 - Grammer Notes

Posted on 2017-09-24

1 To do : n./adj./adv. – Subject,Object,Predicative,Attribute,Adverbial

To love for the sake of being loved is human.(Subject)

It’s impossible for us to master English in a short time.(Subject)

help (to) do sth.(Object)

ability ** to do ** (Attribute)

He is the best man to choose.(Attribute)
He is the best man to be chosen.(被选择)
He is the best man to make a choice.(去选择)

To avoid criticism,do nothing,say nothing,be nothing.(O-Adverbial)

He lived to be a hundred years.(R-Adverbial)

2 V-ING V-ED adj./adv. – Attribute,Adverbial,Complement

V-ING

Signs barring cell-phone use are a familiar sight to anyone.(Attribute)

Unidentified Flying Object
=unidentified objects that fly

falling leaves
=leaves are falling

Waiting for a bus, a brick fell on my head.(Wrong)
A brick fell on my head while I was waiting for a bus.(Correct)

V-ED

a respected writer
=a writer who is respected

boiled water
water that has been boiled

I fell in love with you at the first sight.(修饰fell)
