Posted on 2017-11-14

2015.12 - 2

C

  1. freight
    (1) Freight is the movement of goods by trucks, trains, ships, or aeroplanes.
    (2) Freight is goods that are transported by trucks, trains, ships, or aeroplanes.

  2. merit
    (1) The merits of something are its advantages or other good points.
    (2) If someone or something merits a particular action or treatment, they deserve it.

  3. deserve
    (1) If you say that a person or thing deserves something, you mean that they should have it or receive it because of their actions or qualities.

  4. intensive

  5. permeate

  6. echo n./v.
    (1) An echo is a sound caused by a noise being reflected off a surface such as a wall.
    (2) If you echo someone’s words, you repeat them or express agreement with their attitude or opinion.
    (3) A detail or feature that reminds you of something else can be referred to as an echo.

  1. address(+1) v. 称呼

  2. term(s)
    (1) 时期
    (2) 术语
    (3) 称为
    (4) 条款
    (5) in terms of

D

  1. sanction
    (1) legal sanction
    (2) without the sanction of the government
  2. watered-down
  3. curriculum
  4. overly adv. 过度地；极度地
  5. diluted
    (1) If a liquid is diluted or dilutes, it is added to or mixes with water or another liquid, and becomes weaker.
    (2) If someone or something dilutes a belief, quality, or value, they make it weaker and less effective.
  6. superiority
    (1) If one side in a war or conflict has superiority, it has an advantage over its enemy, for example because it has more soldiers or better equipment.
